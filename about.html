Welcome to Volunteering made easy!

If you have always wanted to dedicate some time to volunteering, but don't know where to start, you are in the right place!


About the website

Volunteering Made Easy collects all the available volunteering opportunities in London, all divided into categories, to make your search easier. What are you waiting for? Just select a category from our homepage and get clicking!


About us

This website was born from Alice's and Nadia's passion for volunteering

Alice has a BSc in Neuroscience from the University of St Andrews and is now pursuing an MSc in Cancer here in London, at University College London. She has always been very active in the volunteering community, being involved in fundraisers and other initiatives with Breast Cancer Now, The Princess Trust, the St Andrews Voluntary Service and local hospitals.


Nadia has a BSc in Psychology from the University of Bristol and is now completing an MSc in Child Development at University College London. Nadia has been an active volunteer for over 4 years, volunteering with children and young people to help further their education, with organisations like IntoUniversity and Volunteering Matters, and organisations promoting mental health care.
